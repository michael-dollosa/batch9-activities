Capulus is the latin for coffee. We are a cofee company established in 1882. It is specialized in serving handcrafted coffee in 20 locations in the U.S. states of Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Montana, California and Arizona, and one location in Japan.

Our mission is to create and provide exceptional coffees, roast them to perfection and then use the profits as a way to fund works of compassion in Third World coffee producing nations like the Philippines.

The sip of freshness

We pride ourselves with the products that we create. Our coffee delights the senses with the taste of ripe, chocolate-toned fruit without a hint of sharpness or acidity. Moderate aging further rounds its flavor while developing body that is deep, creamy and full.

Enhancing Life around great coffee - we make sure that customers feel relaxed in our spaces. We have different areas for reservations. Anyone who wishes to have a good time without worrying about the space is perfect here.

We make it better

Our coffee is so smooth you wouldn’t know it is decaffeinated! A blend of some of the best coffee growing regions of the world. While our business is customer-centric, we make sure our employees are well trained and properly compensated.

Customer feedback matters. We make sure that our service is always grounded on our customer's feedback. Our company makes sure that we are not just giving the best product, but also a well thought service.
